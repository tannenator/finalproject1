Cosmo Whitehead

Cosmo was a great dog, loved by everyone who knew him. He was a golden doodle, a mix between a golden retriever and a poodle, and about 3 feet tall. He loved to go on walks in the forest, and run down the squiggly wiggly as fast as possible. In his younger days he would run alongside me as I mountain biked in the forest behind my house. He would also sometimes pull me on my scooter. He lived for 11 years.
